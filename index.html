Because I love you

Bily is my mother-in-law. My wife and I have been caring for her through her battle with cancer.

During this time, we found managing medicines & diet to be confusing & stressful. One weekend, I created an app that would let me track medicines - and that itself was such a blessing!

So, we thought - "What if there was a gorgeous, calming app that could track the medications, diet and vitals for someone you are caring for?"

   The Bily app is being built with by the community on Assembly. Read more about how Assembly helps us bring together the finest people across the world to build a product, and come join us to help us build Bily!
